# Code of Conduct

WuwaPal is a fan-made tool. Keep the community friendly and practical.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent; focus on solutions.
- Respect privacy and personal boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, doxxing, or unwanted sexual content.
- Spam, scams, or disruptive behavior.

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and community spaces tied to
the project.

## Reporting

Report issues to the project team via the WuwaPal Discord server:
https://discord.gg/DFKG4nqUD4

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation as needed. Severity and
repeat behavior will guide the response.
